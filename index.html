Who We Are

Mount Zion Multipurpose Centre is a faith-based non-profit dedicated to uplifting communities through health, empowerment. We work tirelessly to address socio-economic challenges with love and integrity.

What We Do

At Mount Zion Multipurpose Centre, we are committed to creating lasting change through:

  • Delivering health education and stigma reduction for HIV, TB, STIs, and more.
  • Supporting adolescents and youth programs (AYP) to build a brighter future.
  • Addressing gender-based violence (GBVF) and promoting empowerment.
  • Collaborating with stakeholders to reduce poverty and mentor local organizations.

Our Impact

We relentlessly champion accessible healthcare, poverty alleviation, and the defense of human rights. Our transformative work has uplifted countless lives, igniting hope and strengthening communities with enduring resilience.
