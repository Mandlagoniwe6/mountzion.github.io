Our Values

At Mount Zion Multipurpose Centre, we stand united against gender-based violence, guided by the principles of Ubuntu—compassion and humanity for all. Our work is rooted in integrity, honesty, ethics, and love, ensuring every action reflects our commitment to dignity and justice.

These values shape our approach, fostering trust and collaboration as we empower communities and uplift the vulnerable.

Our Objectives

We pursue clear, actionable goals to drive meaningful impact:

    Provide counseling and care for GBVF survivors to heal and rebuild their lives.

    Challenge and reshape societal attitudes toward gender-based violence through education and advocacy.

    Create employment opportunities through agriculture and crafts, promoting self-sufficiency.

    Offer shelter and comprehensive care for vulnerable individuals, ensuring safety and support.

Join Our Efforts

Be a part of our journey to uphold these values and achieve our objectives. Whether through support, volunteering, or spreading the word, your involvement makes a difference.
